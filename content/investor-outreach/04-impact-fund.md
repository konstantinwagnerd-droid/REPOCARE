## Body (170 Worte)

Hi {firstName},

I'm Konstantin, founder of CareAI. We're tackling the elderly-care crisis in DACH from the one angle that compounds: **giving nurses their time back**.

**The impact case:**
- Germany alone will be short 190,000 nurses by 2030 (Federal Statistical Office). Documentation burden is the #1 driver in BGW's "why I'm leaving" survey.
- Our pilots recovered an average of **52 minutes per shift** — time returning to bedside care. That's measurable impact on SDG 3 (Good Health) and SDG 8 (Decent Work).
- Net Promoter Score from nursing staff (not management): **68**. For comparison, care-tech industry median sits at -4.

**The business case (because impact needs to scale):**
- 14 paying pilots, €380k ARR run-rate, 100% retention
- LTV:CAC 1:20, 82% gross margin — scalable unit economics
- Opening seed round €2.5M on €12M pre — SFDR Article 9-eligible thesis

I'd love 30 minutes to share the impact measurement framework (we track both operational and qualitative KPIs quarterly) and see whether {fund} could be a lead or follow-on investor.

Deck + impact thesis: careai.de/impact/{utmToken}.

Best,
Konstantin

## Hot-Take-Varianten

1. **Measurement-rigor:** „Wir messen Impact-KPIs quartalsweise — nicht nur „wir helfen Menschen", sondern minutengenau Zeit-am-Bett, Fehler-Reduktion, Burnout-Scores via validierten Fragebogen."
2. **Diversity-Angle:** „85% unserer Nutzer:innen sind Frauen, ein signifikanter Anteil mit Migrations-Hintergrund. Technologie, die diese Gruppe tatsächlich erreicht, ist selten — und sozial hochwirksam."
3. **Systemic-change-Framing:** „Pflegequalität hängt an Zeit, nicht an Geld. Software, die Zeit zurückgibt, ist systemische Intervention."
